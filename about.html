I was born in Raiganj - a small town in northern West Bengal. But I lived in Kolkata from the time I was six months old till I came to the US for pursuing my Ph.D degree at UC Santa Cruz in 2020. I attended Kendriya Vidyalaya No-2 Salt Lake from standard 1 till 12 and graduated from school on 2015. I stood first in my school. I just graduated with my doctoral degree (Ph.D) in Astronomy from University of California, Santa Cruz. I did my bachelors and masters in Physics from Presidency University, Kolkata. I work broadly on exoplanetary atmospheres. During my undergrad, I have also worked on modelling of flows and emissions around black-holes and variable stars in the Andromeda galaxy.

I love music. I can play a variety of Indian percussion like Tabla, Pakhwaj, Dholak, Naal and Bhangra dhol. I have accompanied many singers and groups with these instruments. I love Indian instrumental classical music. Sitar and Sharod recitals are my favorite. I love acting in plays. I have acted in several plays with various kinds of roles. I am completely detached from acting now with all the busy academic schedules but I am up for it anytime I will get a chance.
